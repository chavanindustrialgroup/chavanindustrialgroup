About Us

Chavan Industrial Group (CIG) is a trusted partner to Indian Railways, the Ministry of Defence and other national institutions, delivering precision supplies, turnkey projects and specialised engineering services.

With decades of expertise, CIG has earned a reputation for reliability in sourcing, manufacturing and delivering high‑quality materials and components. Our engineers design and construct robust solutions tailored to the demanding environments of railway and defence infrastructures.

By blending meticulous planning, innovative technology and agile project management, we ensure success from start to finish. Whether executing large‑scale infrastructure upgrades or providing specialised equipment, CIG is committed to shaping India’s future.
